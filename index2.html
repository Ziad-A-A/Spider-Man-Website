Andrew-Garfield

"The Amazing Spider-Man" (2012) follows Peter Parker, a high school outcast raised by his Uncle Ben and Aunt May after being abandoned by his parents. Discovering a mysterious briefcase belonging to his father, Peter investigates his parents' disappearance, leading him to Oscorp and Dr. Curt Connors, his father's former partner. After being bitten by a genetically altered spider, Peter gains spider-like abilities and becomes Spider-Man. His journey intertwines with his budding romance with Gwen Stacy and a confrontation with Connors, who transforms into the Lizard after experimenting with a serum. Peter must use his powers to stop the Lizard's plan to mutate New York City, facing personal loss and growth along the way.
